## Setup and Initial Configuration

Get started with Inferno Bot in just a few simple steps.

### Initial Setup

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start the bot" icon="robot">
    Open the Inferno bot (@infernotrade_bot) and run the `/start` command to begin your trading journey.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Make your first deposit" icon="wallet">
    After running `/start`, you will be prompted with your wallet address. Deposit Solana (SOL) to your provided address to fund your trading account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Trading Operations

Learn how to view available trades and manage your positions effectively.

### Available Trades

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Trading Offers" icon="list" href="/commands">
    Use the `/offers` command to list current trading offers available for trading.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Trading Interface" icon="chart-line" href="https://www.infernobot.io/tokens">
    Visit the web interface for a complete view of available trades and market data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Opening Positions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Execute a long position" icon="trend-up">
    To open a long position on a cryptocurrency from the available list:

    1. Use the command: `/Long [Ticker]`
    2. Example: `/Long gor`
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Set leverage and amount" icon="sliders">
    1. Select your desired leverage when prompted
    2. Enter the trade amount:
       - `1` = 1 SOL
       - `0.1` = 0.1 SOL
    3. Confirm your order to execute the trade
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Position Management

Monitor and close your trading positions with ease.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
